Frequently Asked Questions

How do I purchase a card?

Click "View Details" on any card to be taken directly to our eBay listing. All purchases are processed securely through eBay's platform.

Are all cards authentic?

Yes! We take authenticity seriously. Graded cards come with certification from PSA, SGC, or BGS. All cards are carefully inspected before listing.

How fast do you ship?

We ship within 1-2 business days of payment. All cards are carefully packaged to ensure they arrive in perfect condition.

Can I request specific cards?

Absolutely! Send us a message on Instagram or Twitter with what you're looking for, and we'll do our best to help you find it.
