Your Rights

1. Introduction

Welcome to Collectors Playbook ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you visit our website or make a purchase from us.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, such as when you:

  • Browse our card listings
  • Make a purchase through our eBay store
  • Contact us for support
  • Sign up for updates (when available)

Types of Information:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, shipping address, and payment information (processed through eBay)
  • Transaction Information: Details about cards purchased and transaction history
  • Communication Data: Any messages you send to us

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Process and fulfill your orders
  • Communicate with you about your purchases
  • Provide customer support
  • Send updates about new card listings (with your consent)
  • Improve our website and services
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. We may share your information with:

  • eBay: For processing transactions and orders
  • Shipping Partners: To deliver your cards
  • Legal Authorities: When required by law or to protect our rights

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. Transactions are processed through eBay's secure platform.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information
  • Opt-out of marketing communications

7. Children's Privacy

While Collectors Playbook is operated by a young entrepreneur, we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent. All transactions must be completed by adults or with parental supervision.

8. Third-Party Links

Our website contains links to eBay and social media platforms. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third-party sites. Please review their privacy policies separately.

9. Cookies

We use cookies to enhance your browsing experience and analyze site traffic. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page with an updated effective date.

11. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our practices, please contact us through:

  • Our eBay store: collectors-playbook-tx
  • Instagram: @collectorsplaybook
  • Twitter/X: @CPlaybookTX

Note: As we primarily operate through eBay, many aspects of data collection and processing are governed by eBay's policies.

We encourage you to review eBay's Privacy Policy as well.

Your privacy matters to us. We are committed to protecting your personal information and being transparent about our practices.
